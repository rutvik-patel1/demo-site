Welcome to Celestis Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd

Celestis Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. is a research driven company in the field of Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient (API), API intermediates and impurities related to the API. It provides the synthetic chemistry technology to customers for API synthesis.

Celestis Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. will provide world-class R & D solutions in the field of API research with cost effective way.

Celestis can synthesize specialized small organic compounds which are not available easily.

Celestis have expertise in isolation and characterization of natural products.

Our aim is to be research driven R&D organization.
